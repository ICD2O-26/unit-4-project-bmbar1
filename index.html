What is Android app developing?

Android app development is the process of creating software applications running the android operating system.

How does it work?

Android app development involves writting code in languages such as Kotlin or Java within and IDE called Android Studio.

Social impacts

Positive impacts

Android app development has improved communication, learning, and access to information. It supports important areas like education, healthcare, and business by making services faster and more accessible.

Negative impacts

Android apps can sometimes raise privacy concerns if they collect user data without clear permission. Some low-quality or harmful apps may mislead users.
